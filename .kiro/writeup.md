## How Kiro was used in ForeverHome AI

Kiro was instrumental in accelerating the development of ForeverHome AI within the 14-day hackathon window. As a solo developer, Kiro acted as a highly effective pair-programmer, helping me achieve a premium level of polish and complex functionality that would normally require a full team. 

Key areas where Kiro assisted:
1. **Premium UI/UX Implementation:** Kiro helped transition the app's styling to a modern aesthetic, incorporating complex CSS variables for glassmorphism panels, dynamic hover micro-animations, and responsive layout designs using Tailwind CSS.
2. **AI Integration:** Kiro assisted in writing robust failover logic for the Gemini API integration in `src/lib/gemini.ts`, ensuring that the 9 Lives Protocol AI Coach remained highly reliable.
3. **PWA Configuration:** Kiro guided the setup of `next-pwa`, handling manifest generation and caching strategies to ensure the web app felt like a native, installable mobile application.
4. **Security Auditing:** Kiro helped write and audit the `firestore.rules` to ensure strict Role-Based Access Control, particularly the write-only configuration for sensitive AI logs.

By leveraging Kiro, I was able to focus on the high-level product vision and architecture, while iterating incredibly quickly on implementation details.
